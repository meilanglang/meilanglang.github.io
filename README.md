## About

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Macau / Hangzhou Institute of Medicine, Chinese Academy of Sciences. My research focuses on:

- mRNA design
- RNA structure prediction
- RNA language models
- RNA-RNA interaction prediction
- mRNA modeling and regulatory sequence analysis

## Selected Projects

### 3UTRESM

A 3′UTR foundation model for mRNA regulatory tasks, including mRNA degradation prediction, RNA modification prediction and RNA-protein interaction prediction.

### CodonMamba

A bidirectional Mamba-based codon language model for host-adaptive codon optimization and mRNA sequence design.

### RNA-MSM

A multiple sequence alignment-based RNA language model for RNA structural inference.

### SPOT-RNAc

A benchmark and prediction framework for RNA-RNA complex interactions.
